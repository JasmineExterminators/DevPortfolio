Custom Telemetry PiHat

This is a custom raspberry PI hat. This custom PCB was used to process analog and digital outputs from tire temperature, time-of-flight, and linear potentiometer sensors. After processing the data, the PiHat stores it on the Pi itself and we SSH into the Pi to retrieve telemetry data in real-time.
